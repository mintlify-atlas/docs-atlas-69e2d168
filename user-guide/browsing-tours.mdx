Okulink Safaris offers multiple ways to discover and explore safari tours. Whether you're looking for specific categories of safaris or browsing all available options, this guide will help you find your perfect adventure.

## Viewing All Tours

The main safari listing page displays all available tours in the system.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Safaris Page">
    Navigate to `/safaris` to view all available safari tours
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Tour Cards">
    Tours are displayed in a grid or list format, showing:
    - Tour name (hotel/destination)
    - Tour image
    - Pricing information (per person sharing, single room rates)
    - Meal inclusions
    - Transport details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a Tour">
    Click on any tour card to view its complete details and booking options
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browsing by Category

Tours are organized into categories to help you find specific types of safari experiences.

<Info>
Categories are defined by the safari organizers and may include options like Wildlife Safaris, Cultural Tours, Adventure Packages, and more.
</Info>

### How to Filter by Category

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Category">
    From the home page or navigation menu, browse the available tour categories
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Category Tours">
    Navigate to `/tours/{category}` where `{category}` is the category name. This displays all tours in that category
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Category Information">
    The category page shows:
    - All tours in the selected category
    - Category description and information
    - Related blog posts for additional context
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browsing by Subcategory

For more refined browsing, tours can be filtered by subcategories within main categories.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Subcategory View">
    Navigate to `/tours/categories/{sub_category}` where `{sub_category}` is the specific subcategory name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Filtered Tours">
    View all tours that match the selected subcategory criteria
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Subcategory Details">
    The page displays:
    - Tours matching the subcategory
    - Subcategory-specific information
    - Recommended blog content
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Tour Details

Each tour has a dedicated details page accessible by clicking on the tour from any listing.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tour Information" icon="info-circle">
    View complete details including destination, accommodation, meals, and activities
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Pricing Details" icon="dollar-sign">
    See per-person sharing rates and single room supplement costs
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Transport Information" icon="van-shuttle">
    Learn about included transportation and transfer details
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Meal Inclusions" icon="utensils">
    Check which meals are included in the tour package
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Tour Information Display

When viewing tours, you'll find the following information:

- **Hotel/Accommodation**: Where you'll be staying during the safari
- **Transport**: How you'll travel during the tour
- **Pricing**: Cost per person sharing and single room rates
- **Meals**: What meals are included in the package
- **Tour Images**: Visual representation of the destination and experience
- **Category & Subcategory**: Classification of the tour type

<Tip>
Tours are displayed with the most recent additions appearing first, helping you discover new safari experiences as they become available.
</Tip>

## Related Content

While browsing tours, you'll also see:

- **Blog Posts**: Recent articles related to safari experiences and travel tips
- **Related Tours**: Similar tours you might be interested in
- **Videos**: Visual content showcasing safari experiences

<Note>
All tour prices and availability are subject to change. For the most current information, proceed to the booking page or contact Okulink Safaris directly.
</Note>
