Your user account is essential for making bookings and managing your safari experiences on Okulink Safaris. This guide covers everything from registration to profile management.

## Creating an Account

To create a new user account on Okulink Safaris:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration">
    Navigate to the registration page through the login/signup link in the navigation menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Registration Form">
    Provide the required information:
    - Name
    - Email address
    - Password
    - Password confirmation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click the register button to create your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Email Verification">
    After registration, you'll be redirected to the email verification notice page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Okulink Safaris uses Laravel's built-in authentication system with email verification enabled for enhanced security.
</Info>

## Email Verification

Email verification is required before you can make bookings or access certain features.

### Verification Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    After registration, check your inbox for a verification email from Okulink Safaris
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Verification Link">
    Click the verification link in the email. The link format is: `/email/verify/{id}/{hash}`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Verification">
    The system automatically verifies your email and redirects you to the homepage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Booking">
    You can now access all features including making safari bookings
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Didn't Receive the Email?

If you didn't receive your verification email:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Verification Page">
    Navigate to `/email/verify` while logged in
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request New Link">
    Click the "Resend Verification Email" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rate Limiting">
    You can request up to 6 verification emails per minute (throttled for security)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You'll see the message: "Verification link sent!" when successful
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The verification link is signed and secure. Don't share it with others as it's specific to your account.
</Warning>

## Logging In

Once your account is created and verified:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Login Page">
    Click the login link in the navigation menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide your email address and password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login">
    Click the login button to access your account
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Remember your credentials securely. Use a password manager for better security.
</Tip>

### Accessing Your Profile

Navigate to `/profile` while logged in to view:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Personal Information" icon="user">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Booking History" icon="list">
    All your past and current safari bookings
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Booking Status" icon="circle-check">
    Track whether bookings are pending or confirmed

<Note>
You must be logged in and have a verified email to access the profile page. The route is protected by both `auth` and `verified` middleware.

## Managing Your Profile

You can update your account information through the user update interface.

### Updating Account Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Update Page">
    Navigate to `/users/update-user/{id}` where `{id}` is your user ID
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Information">
    You can modify:
    - Name
    - Email address
    - Password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Password Confirmation">
    If changing your password, enter it twice to confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Submit the form to update your credentials
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

<Info>
When updating your password:
- Enter the new password in the password field
- Re-enter the same password in the confirmation field
- Both fields must match exactly
</Info>

<Warning>
If passwords don't match, you'll see the error: "Password does not match" and changes won't be saved.
</Warning>

## Viewing Your Bookings

Your profile displays all bookings associated with your account.

### Booking Information Display

For each booking, you can view:

- **Destination**: The safari tour you booked
- **Travel Dates**: Your arrival date
- **Party Size**: Number of adults and kids
- **Contact Details**: Phone number and email
- **Special Requirements**: Any special requests you submitted
- **Transaction Code**: Your payment reference
- **Booking Status**: Whether the booking is confirmed or pending

<Tip>
Bookings are paginated with 10 bookings per page and sorted by most recent first, making it easy to find your latest reservations.
</Tip>

Passwords are stored securely:
- Hashed using Laravel's Hash facade
- Never stored in plain text
- Cannot be retrieved (only reset)

## Account Status

### Required for Bookings

To make bookings, your account must be:

1. **Registered**: You've created an account
2. **Authenticated**: You're currently logged in
3. **Verified**: Your email address has been verified

<Warning>
Attempting to access booking features without meeting all three requirements will redirect you to the appropriate page (login or verification).
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't access profile or bookings">
    **Solution**: Ensure you're logged in and have verified your email address. Check your inbox for the verification email or request a new one.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Password update not working">
    **Solution**: Make sure both password fields match exactly. Check for spaces or typos.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Verification email not received">
    **Solution**: Check your spam folder, then request a new verification email from `/email/verify`. You can request up to 6 emails per minute.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't log in">
    **Solution**: Verify you're using the correct email and password. If forgotten, use the password reset feature.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
