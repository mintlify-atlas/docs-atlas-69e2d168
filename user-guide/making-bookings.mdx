Booking a safari tour on Okulink Safaris is a straightforward process. This guide walks you through each step from selecting your tour to receiving confirmation.

## Prerequisites

Before you can complete a booking, you need:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User Account" icon="user">
    You must be registered and logged in to make a booking
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Verified Email" icon="envelope-circle-check">
    Your email address must be verified before booking
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
Booking routes are protected by authentication and email verification middleware. Attempting to book without being logged in or verified will redirect you to the login or verification page.
</Warning>

## The Booking Process

### Step 1: Select Your Tour

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Tours">
    Navigate through the available tours using the browsing methods described in the [Browsing Tours](/user-guide/browsing-tours) guide
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Destination">
    Click on the tour you wish to book to view its details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Booking">
    Click the "Book Now" button or navigate to `/bookings/create/{destination}` where `{destination}` is the name of your chosen tour
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Fill in Booking Details

Once you're on the booking form, you'll need to provide the following information:

#### Required Information

<Info>
All fields marked as required must be completed to submit your booking.
</Info>

**Personal Details:**
- First Name (required)
- Last Name (required)
- Phone Number (required)
- Email Address (required, must be entered twice for confirmation)
- Country

**Tour Details:**
- Arrival Date (required)
- Number of Adults (required)
- Number of Kids (required)
- Destination (pre-filled from selected tour)

**Payment Information:**
- Transaction Code (required)

**Additional Information:**
- Special Requirements (optional)

<Tip>
Use the "Special Requirements" field to communicate any dietary restrictions, accessibility needs, or specific requests for your safari tour.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Email Confirmation

The booking form requires you to enter your email address twice to ensure accuracy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Type your email address in the first email field
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Email">
    Re-enter the same email address in the confirmation field
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Validation">
    The system will verify that both email addresses match before allowing submission
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If the email addresses don't match, you'll receive an error message: "please provide matching emails" and will need to correct the information.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Complete CAPTCHA Verification

For security purposes, you must complete a CAPTCHA verification before submitting your booking.

<Note>
The reCAPTCHA helps protect against automated booking attempts and ensures genuine customer bookings.
</Note>

### Step 5: Submit Your Booking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Information">
    Double-check all entered information for accuracy
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Form">
    Click the submit button to process your booking
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Upon successful submission, you'll be redirected to your profile page with a success message: "Booking has been successful, We will contact you within 24hrs!"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Booking

### View Your Bookings

Once your booking is submitted, you can view it in your profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile">
    Navigate to `/profile` while logged in
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Booking History">
    Your profile page displays all your bookings, with the most recent bookings appearing first
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Booking Details">
    Each booking shows:
    - Destination
    - Arrival date
    - Number of adults and kids
    - Booking status
    - Special requirements
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Booking Confirmation Process

<Info>
After submitting your booking, the Okulink Safaris team will review and confirm your reservation within 24 hours.
</Info>

The booking goes through the following states:
- **Submitted** (Status: 0): Your booking has been received and is pending review
- **Confirmed** (Status: 1): Your booking has been confirmed by the team

## Managing Your Bookings

### Editing a Booking

If you need to modify your booking before it's confirmed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Booking">
    Navigate to `/bookings/edit/{id}` where `{id}` is your booking ID
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Information">
    Modify the necessary details (first name, last name, email, phone number, arrival date, adults, kids)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Submit the updated information
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
You must be logged in and have email verification to edit bookings.
</Warning>

### Canceling a Booking

To cancel a booking:

1. Contact Okulink Safaris directly or
2. Administrators can delete bookings through the admin panel at `/bookings/delete/{id}`

<Note>
For cancellation policies and refund information, please refer to the terms and conditions or contact customer support.
</Note>

## Common Booking Issues

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Not Logged In" icon="lock">
    **Solution**: Create an account or log in before attempting to book
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Email Not Verified" icon="envelope">
    **Solution**: Check your email for the verification link and verify your account
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Email Mismatch" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    **Solution**: Ensure both email fields contain the exact same email address
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="CAPTCHA Failed" icon="shield">
    **Solution**: Complete the CAPTCHA verification correctly
  </Card>

## Next Steps

After your booking is confirmed:

1. **Receive Confirmation**: You'll be contacted within 24 hours via email or phone
2. **Payment Processing**: Complete any additional payment requirements as instructed
3. **Pre-Safari Information**: Receive detailed information about your safari
4. **Enjoy Your Safari**: Arrive on your scheduled date and enjoy your adventure!

<Tip>
Keep your transaction code handy for reference when communicating with the Okulink Safaris team about your booking.
</Tip>
