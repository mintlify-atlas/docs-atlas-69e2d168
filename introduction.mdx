<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold text-gray-900 dark:text-white mb-4">Quick start</h2>
  <p className="text-base text-gray-600 dark:text-gray-400 mb-8">
    Get up and running with Okulink Safaris in minutes
  </p>

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Create your account">
      Register for an account to start exploring safari tours and making bookings.
      
      ```bash
      POST /register
      {
        "name": "John Doe",
        "email": "john@example.com",
        "password": "secure_password"
      }
      ```
      
      <Note>Email verification is required before you can make bookings.</Note>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Browse available tours">
      Explore our curated collection of safari tours across Kenya's most spectacular destinations.
      
      ```bash
      GET /safaris
      ```
      
      Filter by category, duration, or price to find the perfect safari experience.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Make your first booking">
      Select your preferred tour and complete the booking process.
      
      ```bash
      POST /bookings/new
      {
        "tour_id": 1,
        "first_name": "John",
        "last_name": "Doe",
        "email": "john@example.com",
        "phone_number": "+254712345678",
        "adults": 2,
        "kids": 1
      }
      ```
      
      <Info>You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details.</Info>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Manage your bookings">
      View and manage all your bookings from your profile dashboard.
      
      ```bash
      GET /profile
      ```
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
